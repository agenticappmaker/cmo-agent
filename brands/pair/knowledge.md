# Pair — Brand Knowledge

## What is Pair?
Pair is a wine and food pairing app that helps you find the perfect match for every pour. Browse hundreds of expertly curated pairings — wine with cheese, chocolate, charcuterie, dinner dishes, and more. Each pairing includes tasting notes, serving suggestions, regional info, and complementary sides.

## Key Features
- **WINE & FOOD PAIRINGS** — Hundreds of curated pairings across red, white, rosé, sparkling, and dessert wines matched with cheeses, chocolates, meats, and dishes from around the world.
- **PAIRING BY WINE** — Pick your bottle and see every food that pairs with it, ranked by match quality.
- **PAIRING BY FOOD** — Start with what you're eating and find the perfect wine to complement it.
- **GLASSWARE GUIDE** — Know which glass to use for every wine type (Bordeaux, Burgundy, Flute, etc.).
- **REGION EXPLORER** — Discover wines by region: Napa Valley, Bordeaux, Tuscany, Rioja, Marlborough, and more.
- **SAVE & SHARE** — Save your favorite pairings and share curated menus for dinner parties.
- **iOS app** — free, coming soon to the App Store.

## Brand Voice
- Warm and inviting — wine is about connection and pleasure
- Knowledgeable but never pretentious — we demystify wine
- Visual — every post should make people want to open a bottle
- Lifestyle-oriented — pairings are about the moment, not just the glass
- Confident recommendations — we tell you exactly what works and why

## Content That Performs Well
- Beautiful wine + cheese/food photography with pairing notes in caption
- "Tonight's pairing" — a specific wine + food combo with mood/occasion
- Regional spotlights (e.g., "3 Napa Cabs that pair perfectly with aged cheddar")
- Seasonal content (summer rosé + salads, winter reds + stews)
- Quick tips ("Why you should never pair tannic reds with fish")
- Entertaining guides (holiday cheese boards, date night pairings)

## What NOT to Post
- Don't be wine-snobby — no gatekeeping, no "you're drinking it wrong"
- No AI-looking or stock-photo-feeling images
- No long essays — keep captions scannable
- No posts without a specific pairing recommendation
