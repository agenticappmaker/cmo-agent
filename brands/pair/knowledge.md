# Pair — Brand Knowledge

## What is Pair?
Pair is a mobile app that helps you build the perfect cheese, wine, chocolate, and nut pairing board — curated to your occasion and guest count. It's the easy-hosting companion for anyone who wants their board to feel intentional, not accidental.

## Key Features
- **BOARD BUILDER WIZARD** (new) — Tell it the occasion (Date Night · Dinner Party · Picnic · Holiday · Casual Hangout · Tasting Flight) and how many guests. In 3 taps, it suggests a balanced board: 3 cheeses (soft / semi-hard / hard), 2 contrasting wines matched to your cheeses, 1 chocolate, 1 nut — with per-person quantity math. Swap any item with one tap.
- **216 CURATED ITEMS** — 84 cheeses, 73 wines, 41 chocolates, 18 nuts. Each item carries origin, milk type, wine style, and `goesWellWith` relationships used by the pairing engine.
- **MY BOARD** — Save your board, organize named boards, come back to them for repeat occasions.
- **SWAPS TAB** — Suggest substitutes if a cheese or wine isn't at your local shop.
- **NO ACCOUNT REQUIRED FOR CORE USE** — data is local-first with optional cloud sync.

## Brand Voice
- Warm and hosting-minded — we help people make other people feel special
- Curatorial, not encyclopedic — we don't lecture, we suggest
- Unpretentious about wine and cheese — expertise without snobbery
- Celebratory — every board is for *something*, even if it's Tuesday
- Visual — pairs look beautiful; posts must look beautiful

## Content Series (rotate — never repeat back-to-back)

### 🧀 Board Builder Spotlight
Show an auto-curated board from the app for a specific occasion. Caption walks through *why* each piece was chosen (e.g., "The Humboldt Fog needed acidity to cut the bloom, so we matched it with a Sancerre"). Always include a quantity-per-person breakdown and a CTA: "Tap Build a Board for me in Pair."

### 🍷 Pairing 101
Teach the *why* of a single pairing (e.g., "Why Manchego + Tempranillo works" — same region, shared tannic structure, complementary fatty/dry profile). Diagram-style. End with the app CTA: "Pair has 1,200+ of these matches pre-computed."

### 🧀 Cheese of the Week
Deep-dive one cheese: origin, milk, aging, ideal temperature, ideal wine, history. Make it a single beautiful photo + 3 lines of context. Weekly Tuesday.

### 🍷 Wine of the Week
Deep-dive one wine: producer, region, grape, style notes, what cheese it loves, what cheese it hates. Weekly Thursday.

### 🎉 Occasion Boards
Holidays / seasons / cultural moments. Valentine's Day board. Friendsgiving. 4th of July picnic. Dry January (chocolates + nuts + bold non-alc pairings — the sober board).

### 🍫 Chocolate + Nut Pairings
Give the underdog its due. 70% dark + Rioja. Marcona almonds + Fino sherry. Hazelnuts + Port. These posts perform well for the "I didn't know" factor.

### 💡 Host Tips
Micro-how-tos: "How much cheese per person?" (1.5 oz). "Do you actually need a cheese knife for each cheese?" (yes, for soft vs. hard). "Should cheese come straight from the fridge?" (no — 45 min on the counter first).

### ✨ App Feature Showcase
Walk through one feature with a screen recording or polished screenshot. New Board Builder, Swaps tab, My Board list, quantity math, occasion filtering.

## What Performs Well on IG/TikTok in Pair's Category
- Build-the-board timelapse videos (15-30 sec, satisfying layering)
- "What would a sommelier add to this board?" reveal videos
- Overhead flat-lay photography with natural linen and matte wood
- 3-card carousel: "Before board / After board / Why it works"
- Hosting tips that save time (quantity math, shopping list shortcuts)

## What to Avoid
- Alcohol-focused messaging that alienates sober hosts — always mention the alcohol-free pairing path
- Photography where the board is out of focus or poorly lit
- Jargon without a plain-English translation (tannic, reductive, noble rot, washed-rind) — explain in parens
- Duplicate content across platforms — each post should have a platform-native edit
