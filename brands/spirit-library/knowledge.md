# Spirit Library — Brand Knowledge

## What is Spirit Library?
Spirit Library is a mobile app (iOS, coming to Android) for cocktail enthusiasts. It's a curated library of exceptional cocktail recipes that users can save, build menus from, manage shopping lists, and share with friends.

## Key Features
- Browse 60+ curated cocktail recipes with beautiful photography
- Save favourites and build custom menus
- Shopping list — add ingredients directly from any recipe
- Share recipes and menus with friends via text, email, or social
- Find bars near you
- Profile with sync across devices

## Brand Voice
- Confident but never arrogant
- Celebratory — cocktails are about joy, connection, and craft
- Educational — we teach without lecturing
- Visual — every post should make people want to make a drink right now

## Content That Performs Well
- Beautiful cocktail photography with the recipe in the caption
- "Did you know?" spirit facts
- "Tonight's pick" — a cocktail recommendation with mood/occasion
- Behind the bar tips (technique, tools, glassware)
- Seasonal content (summer spritz, winter warmer, etc.)

## What to Avoid
- Anything that encourages excessive drinking
- Overly salesy language
- Generic stock photo energy — content should feel crafted and specific

## Cocktails in the App (sample)
Old Fashioned, Negroni, Espresso Martini, Paper Plane, Mai Tai, Penicillin, Last Word, Daiquiri, Paloma, Aviation, Frida, Bandera, B-52, 20th Century, and 50+ more.
